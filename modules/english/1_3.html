The different types of computer buttons

Computers have a variety of buttons, but some buttons are universal like the power button. Look at the front and back of a system unit. How many computer buttons are there? Each computer is different, so the quantity and look of these buttons will differ from computer to computer. However, the power button can be found in most desktop computers and laptops (see figure 1) [1].

The power button

The power button is a round or square button that powers a computer on and off. Typically, the device powers on when the button is pressed and powers off when it is pressed again. Power buttons are found on all kinds of computers, like desktops, tablets, netbooks, laptops and more [2].

Depending on the type of computer, the power button may be on the front of the system unit, on the back of the monitor, on in the inside when you open the lid of a laptop (see figure 2) [1].

The optical disc drive eject button

The optical disc drive eject button is used to manually open or close the optical disc drive tray [3]. Most desktop computers have an optical disc drive, but most laptops do not (see figure 3).
