Module 3: The Computer Keyboard

Lesson 4: Connecting a Keyboard to the Computer

1. Students will be able to connect a wired and a wireless computer keyboard to a desktop computer.


Video




Connecting a Wired Keyboard

Most wired keyboards use USB cables. A USB cable will have this symbol somewhere on the cable, similar to the above picture. The computer should also have a picture of that symbol next to the port (receptacle) where the cable can be plugged in. Most computers have more than one USB port and it doesn’t matter which one you use. Simply plug the cable into the port. If it doesn’t go easily, you may have the cable upside down. Turn the cable 180 degrees and try again.

Connecting a Wireless Keyboard

Connecting a wireless keyboard to your computer is very similar to a wired keyboard. Instead of plugging the USB cable into the USB port, you will be plugging the USB receiver, like those pictured above, into the port. Once the receiver is plugged in, the wireless keyboard should work. Make sure the keyboard also has good batteries.
