Different Types of Computers

There are many types of computers. But when most people hear the word computer, they think of a desktop computer or laptop. However, computers can come in many shapes and sizes, perform various operations, and serve different functions. Examples of computers include tablets, smartphones, smartwatches, game consoles, TVs, servers and more (see Figure 3)


A Desktop Computer and its Main Parts

A desktop computer (see Figure 4) is a personal computing device designed to fit on top of a typical office desk due to its size and power requirements. Its main parts consist of a system unit, a monitor, a keyboard, and a mouse
