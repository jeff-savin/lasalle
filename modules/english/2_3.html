Mouse Clicks

One of the major ways to use the mouse is through clicking. When you click, you interact with the computer and tell it what you want it to do. In order to click, take your hand and hold the mouse. Then, with your index finger, press and release the left mouse button. This will select an object on the computer screen that you want to interact with. In most contexts, clicking the right mouse button will open a menu with options. To perform a double click, quickly click the left mouse button two times. This action is used to open folders, files, or to start a program on your computer. You can also perform a drag and drop to move icons, folders or files around. To do this, click on a file, folder or icon, and hold the click down. Then, drag the object where you would like to place it.
