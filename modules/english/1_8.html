Video Lesson



What are the Correct Steps to Turn On a Desktop Computer?

Once the desktop computer is set up (assembled), the next step is to turn it on. Here are the general steps to turn on a desktop computer:

  1. Locate the system unit.
  2. Locate the power button on the system unit.
  3. Press the power button.
  4. Locate the computer monitor.
  5. Locate the power button on the computer monitor.
  6. Press the power button.

What are the Correct Steps to Turn Off a Desktop Computer?

Depending on the operating system installed on your desktop computer, the steps to turn it off are as follow:

  1. Locate and left click on the start button.
  2. Locate and left click on the power button.
  3. In the submenu that appears, click on shut down.
