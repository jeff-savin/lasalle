The Different Types of Computer Slots

Computer slots are physical connections that allow the hardware of a computer to be expanded. In other words, computer slots enable users to upgrade their computers (see Figure 28) [31,32].

The SD Card Slot

An SD card slot is a slot that is used to insert a secure digital memory card into a computer, printer, or other device (see Figure 29) [33].

The Expansion Card Slots

An expansion card slot is a connection inside a computer on the motherboard or riser card. It provides an installation point for a hardware expansion card to be installed. For example, if your computer did not come with a video card, you could purchase one and install it here (see Figure 30) [12,34].
