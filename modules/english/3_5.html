Module 3: The Computer Keyboard

Lesson 5: Identify the Home Row

1. Students will be able to recognize the home row of the computer keyboard.


Video



Keyboard Home Row

When typing, there is a correct place to put your fingers, and a correct finger to use to press each key. When you put your hands on a keyboard, your fingers should be above the home row. In this image, the home row can be seen as the highlighted keys. Your thumbs should rest on the space bar at the bottom, your left pointer finger should be on the f key and your right pointer finger should be on the j key. Often a keyboard will have slight bumps on these keys to indicate where to put your fingers without looking. Look at your keyboard and see if it has that.
