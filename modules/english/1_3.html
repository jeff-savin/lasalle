The Different Types of Computer Buttons

Every computer has its own set of buttons, but some buttons are universal like the power button. Look at the front and back of your computer and count the number of buttons you see. Each computer is different, so the buttons will vary from computer to computer. However, the power button can be found in most desktop computers and laptops (see Figure 14) [12].

The Power Button

The power button is a round or square button that powers an electronic device on and off. Typically, the device powers on when the button is pressed and powers off when it is pressed again. Power buttons are found on all kinds of computers, like desktops, tablets, netbooks, laptops and more [18].

Depending on the type of computer you have, the power button may be on the front of the system unit, on the back of the monitor, on the inside when you open the lid of a laptop (see Figure 15) [12].

The Optical Disc Drive Eject Button

The optical disc drive eject button is used to manually open or close the optical disc drive tray [19]. Most desktop computers have an optical disc drive, but most laptops do not (see Figure 16).
