What is a Mouse?

A computer mouse is a hand-held pointing device that can understand movements when placed on a surface. It is a main input device for the computer. It can detect forward/backward as well as left/right movements. This motion is typically translated into the motion of a pointer on the computer monitor, which allows for easy control of the computer monitor.

What are the Parts of the Mouse?

The computer mouse consists of the body and three different wants to interact with the computer: the left and right click buttons, and the scroll wheel. The left click button allows you to select objects on the screen, while the right click button will open up context menus with additional operations and selections. The scroll wheel will let you move up and down a page or menu.
